Features

Full Control Over Every Blast

Not just a sound button. Whistle gives you pattern, timing, and volume control — all adjustable live while the app is playing.

🎛️

Pattern Control

Single, double, triple, or rapid-fire. Set the gap between blasts to the millisecond.

🔁

Repeat Cycle

Enable auto-repeat and Whistle loops your pattern continuously until you tap stop.

⏱️

Duration & Volume

Dial in blast length from 0.1s to 2.0s. Adjust volume independently from device level.

🔇

Silent Mode Override

Plays even when your iPhone is on silent. Because a whistle that doesn't blow isn't a whistle.

📵

Fully Offline

No internet required. All sounds are generated on your device. No accounts, no ads.

🎵

Background Audio

Switch apps and Whistle keeps playing. Works with repeat cycles for continuous signaling.
