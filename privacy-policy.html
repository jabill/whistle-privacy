1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Jacked Arts ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the Whistle mobile application ("the App"), available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. By using Whistle, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Because Whistle collects no user data, this policy is brief by design. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what the app does and does not do.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Whistle does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any other identifying information
  • Your device identifier, IP address, or advertising ID
  • Your location (the app requests no location permissions)
  • Audio recordings of any kind (the app plays sounds; it does not record)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral data
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, or photo library
  • Payment or financial information

There is no user account system. You do not create a profile, log in, or provide any information to use the App.

3. How the App Works

Whistle generates whistle sounds entirely on your device using mathematical audio synthesis. All sounds are computed locally in real time — no audio files are downloaded from external servers, and no audio is ever uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

The App uses the following device capabilities:

  • Audio playback: To play synthesized whistle sounds through your device speaker. This is the core function of the App.
  • Background audio: To allow sounds to continue playing when the app is in the background (optional, controlled by you).
  • Haptic feedback: To provide tactile feedback when you interact with controls (iOS only). No data is associated with this.
  • Silent mode override (iOS): The App plays audio even when your iPhone is set to silent, so the whistle works when you need it.

No microphone access is requested or used. The App cannot hear you.

4. Data Storage

Whistle stores a small amount of data locally on your device only — specifically, your last-used settings such as selected whistle type, volume, and pattern preferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not associated with any identifier
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App

We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

5. Third-Party Services

Whistle does not integrate any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs. No third party receives data from or about your use of the App.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Those platforms have their own privacy policies governing your account and device data, which are independent of this policy:

  • Apple Privacy Policy: apple.com/privacy
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

Whistle does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, the App is suitable for use by children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international laws concerning minors' privacy.

8. Your Rights

Because Whistle does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, export, or delete. If you have questions or believe data may have been collected unintentionally, please contact us using the information below and we will investigate promptly.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. Since no personal data is processed, these rights are not practically applicable — but we are committed to transparency and will respond to any inquiry.
